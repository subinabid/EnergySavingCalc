Contract demand

This is your allowed maximum demand. You pay for this irrespective of how much electricity you use. The fixed tariff on the bill is calculated from this number. If you dont use this much, you are paying unwanted charges. If you exceed this limit, there is a penalty. You can get this value from your latest electricity bill.

Total Energy Usage

This is the total energy usage for a month. You pay veriable charges to your utility company based on how much units you sonsume every month. You can get this value from your latest electricity bill.
